PROFILE

Before becoming a full-time educator, I spent over 20 years leading teams and managing operations in the BPO industry. During the pandemic, I chose to pursue my passion for teaching, and it has been one of the most rewarding decisions of my career.

Over the past seven years, I've taught coding, STEM, robotics, and AI to students in the Philippines, Vietnam, the UAE, the UK, and the United States, both online and in the classroom.

I've also worked with students with ADHD and other diverse learning needs, adapting my teaching to meet each learner where they are and helping them build confidence as they grow.


TEACHING PHILOSOPHY

I believe every student has the potential to succeed when they're given the opportunity to learn in a way that works for them.

My classroom is hands-on, project-based, and student-centered. Rather than simply memorizing concepts, students learn by building games, programming robots, exploring artificial intelligence, and solving real-world problems.

I've worked with students who have different learning styles, including students with ADHD and other diverse learning needs. These experiences have taught me that curiosity, creativity, and confidence grow when students feel supported and encouraged to explore.

My goal is to create a learning environment where students feel safe to ask questions, embrace mistakes as part of the learning process, and become independent thinkers who enjoy learning.
